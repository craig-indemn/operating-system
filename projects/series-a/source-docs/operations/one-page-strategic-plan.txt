Indemn One-Page Strategic Plan
Q1 2026

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PURPOSE

Enable effortless customer journeys for insurance. 

We do this by providing natural-language access to information, product configuration, application and policy management.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

MISSION

Revolutionize insurance sales and service through generative AI and interconnected applications, propelling digital transformation across the industry. 

We empower MGAs, agents, and insurers to confidently embrace AI-driven operations in both sales and service journeys. By becoming the go-to platform where AI meets real insurance challenges, we're shaping a more efficient, forward-thinking future for the entire market.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CORE VALUES (We Believe)

1. Technology should simplify insurance.
2. Work should be fun and rewarding.
3. In a mindset of curiosity, adaptability and resilience.
4. GenAI is foundational for Indemn; AI agents will transform digital distribution.
5. Customer-centric, product-led growth (PLG) is key to our success.
6. Partnerships are crucial to transforming the industry.
7. AI should enable amazing customer journeys.
8. Thought leadership is central to our value in the market. In partnering people with AI.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3-YEAR TARGETS

• Define and lead a new category: revenue-driving agentic AI platform built specifically for insurance.
• Develop full online AI agent access for at least 50 digital insurance products
• Define the communication standard for AI in insurance.
• Become recognized as a thought leader for AI in the insurance industry.
• Lead the industry in insurance policies sold by AI agents.

3-Year Metrics:
• 3,000 AI agents deployed
• ≥ 30 customers at ≥ $100,000/month
• ≥ 300 customers @ ≥ $5,000/month
• End-to-end sales support for life and P&C products

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1-YEAR GOALS

• Increase user configuration to AI Agent studio.
• Expand thought leadership positioning & increase publishing rate.
• Increase organic website traffic
• Reduce AI agent deployment time / cost
• Sign & deploy enterprise customers
• Develop dedicated insurance compliance tools in the platform
• Increase % of conversions autonomously resolved
• Expand accessible industry verticals
• Build, document and deploy sales processes for ICPs
• Build tools identified by the sales team to foster new account acquisition
• Working alignment across the company through a comprehensive product development roadmap

Annual Metrics:
• 75 new customers
• Achieve SOC-2, HIPPA, and PCI Compliance
• 10 new customers ≥ $25k + $5k/month
• ≥ 7 new products/integrations
• Ave. implementation cost/customer ≥ $4,000
• ≥ 1,000 AI conversations/week

• Develop comprehensive product development roadmap with tech team
• Scale multimodal AI agents in marketing & sales channels
• Update indemn.ai to better facilitate ICP sales, improve engagement + lead capture
• Consistently identify & engage target enterprise customers

STRATEGIC INITIATIVES

1. Deliver the premier AI purchase experience for insurance.
2. Develop and implement a robust customer feedback engine, enabling rapid personalization at scale.
3. Create complementary software platform partnerships
4. Deploy out-of-the-box and user configurable AI agents for common insurance workflows
5. Introduce platform solutions capable of managing insurance workflows at scale
6. Penetrate international markets
7. Self sign-up
8. AI support agents (Jarvis) for developing and improving AI agents, as well as navigating our software.
9. Land & Expand: consistent use-case expansion with current customers
10. Most secure and compliant AI agents platform in insurance

DEFINITIONS

• CAC – Customer Acquisition Cost
• ICP – Ideal Customer Profile
• PLG - Product Led Growth, a strategy focusing on the use of the product itself as the primary driver of customer acquisition and expansion
• BoH (Back of House) - operations not directly interacting with the end-use customer
• Front of House - end-use customer interactions
• Enterprise Customer - insurance org over $1B in annual premium
