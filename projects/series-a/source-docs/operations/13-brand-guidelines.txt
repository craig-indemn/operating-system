Indemn Brand Guidelines

For any design work, presentations, or materials.


PRIMARY COLORS

Iris (Primary Accent Color)
Hex: #4752a3
Usage: Primary brand color, logo, headlines, buttons, interactive elements
Note: This is a purple-blue, not pure blue

Lilac
Hex: #a67cb7
Usage: Lighter purple accents, hover states, secondary elements

Eggplant (Dark)(Primary Mark Color)
Hex: #1e2553
Usage: Dark backgrounds, dark mode primary, text on light backgrounds

White
Hex: #ffffff
Usage: Light backgrounds, text on dark backgrounds


SUPPORTING COLORS

Lime (Accent)
Hex: #e0da67
Usage: Accent highlights, callouts, emphasis (use sparingly)

Olive
Hex: #2a2b1a
Usage: Supporting dark color, text alternatives


TYPOGRAPHY

Primary Font: Barlow
- Headlines: Barlow Bold (all caps or title case)
- Subheadlines: Barlow Medium
- Body Copy: Barlow Light

NOT Inter - that was used incorrectly in past implementations.


COLOR USAGE

On Eggplant background: White text (body), Lilac (large text only), Lime (accents)
On Iris background: White text (body), Eggplant (large text only)
On White background: Iris text, Eggplant text
On Lilac background: Eggplant text (body), White (large text only)


LOGO

Primary: Logomark (icon) + "Indemn" wordmark
Safe space: Half the size of the logo mark around all sides
Colors: Iris on white, White on Iris/Eggplant


IMPORTANT NOTES

1. The official primary color is Iris (#4752a3) - a purple-blue, not pure blue
2. Font is Barlow, not Inter
3. Eggplant (#1e2553) is the dark background color, not slate
4. Lime (#e0da67) is for accent highlights
5. The brand has a purple/violet feel, not a pure blue feel
