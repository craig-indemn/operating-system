5. The Proposal is the spine — hydrated continuously, rendered at the end

The Proposal entity exists from discovery onward, starting empty. Every touchpoint adds more structured data to it. At proposal stage there's enough to render the final document. This is the culmination — every earlier artifact was building toward it.
