# Indemn OS — Customer System Roadmap

*Shared with Kyle from our 2026-04-28 sync. Captures what we aligned on. Living document — revisit as the work moves.*

## The frame

The Indemn OS is the platform. The customer system is the first thing built on it — and it's how Indemn runs its own sales-and-success motion. Every customer becomes a constellation: Company, Contacts, Touchpoints, Operations, Opportunities, Proposal, Phases. The team and AI both operate on the same model. The Proposal is the spine; every stage of the sales motion produces an artifact via the same mechanism.

## Where we are

Phase A is done. We traced **GR Little** end-to-end through DISCOVERY and produced a follow-up email artifact (validated 4/24). We traced **Alliance** end-to-end through PROPOSAL and produced the v2 proposal doc (in Cam's Drive folder, 4/28). Mechanism proven across two stages. Foundation infrastructure — LangSmith tracing, evaluations, OS bug convergence — running in parallel.

## What ships next

1. **Customer-snapshot view** — every customer's constellation queryable. The team's daily home.
2. **Voice-dump ingestion** — microphone → meeting entity. Lead capture from cell-phone calls.
3. **Armadillo, Amenta, TFG** run through end-to-end. Results back to Kyle for review.
4. **Proposal generator** — iterating from the Alliance v2 baseline.
5. **Playbook hydration from historical data** — process the 1-month backfill, analyze patterns by stage, draft the playbook from what's actually happened. Use it, iterate, refine.

## Who uses what

- **Kyle — guinea pig.** Daily use starting end of this week (target) or within 2 weeks. Insurance-domain truth. Lead entry via voice-dump.

- **Full historical hydration** — past 1 month, after team-use proves stability.
