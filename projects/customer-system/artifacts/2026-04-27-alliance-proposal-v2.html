Unlocking Revenue Capacity

Alliance Insurance manages a book of 9,516 accounts and 16,744 policies, including roughly 3,946 renewing in the next 90 days. The current challenge is not a lack of effort — it is the operational lift required to deliver consistent renewal stewardship at scale across your full Producer book.

Indemn provides an "Insurance-Native" digital workforce. This v2 proposal centers on a single, high-leverage wedge: a Retention Associate that automates renewal stewardship for every Producer, every renewal. Once that's proven, we expand to lead capture and broader service automation.

Addressing the Implementation Gap

We recognize the bandwidth constraint with nine new hires absorbing your team's onboarding capacity. This roadmap is designed to deliver value with minimal change-absorption.

  • Wedge-first. Phase 1 sits behind your Producers — no customer-facing rollout, no new tools to learn. The Retention Associate works inside your existing renewal cadence.
  • Human-in-the-Loop. Every snapshot and pre-review email surfaces to the Producer for review and one-click send. No "hallucinations" reach clients.
  • Insurance-Native. Pre-trained on binding authority, surplus lines logic, NC-specific regulations, and your specific carrier list.

The Three-Phase Roadmap

Phase 1: WEDGE (Months 1–2) — Renewal Stewardship

Focus: Producer-led renewals, automated snapshots and pre-review emails.

  • The Retention Associate (compound): Inbox + Knowledge + custom orchestration that assembles per-renewal snapshots from BT Core and drafts pre-review emails.
  • Outcome: Stewardship prep cut from ~30 minutes to ~5 minutes per renewal.
  • Investment: $2,500/month (Month-to-month).

Phase 2: WALK (Months 3–4) — Lead Capture

Focus: Stop the bleeding of missed inbound calls.

  • The Lead Capture Associate (bilingual): A Spanish + Vietnamese voice and chat Associate that ensures every inbound inquiry is answered.
  • Outcome: Reduce 100+ missed calls/week to fewer than 10.
  • Investment: $2,500/month (Month-to-month).

Phase 3: RUN (Months 5+) — Full Digital Workforce

Focus: Strategic Control and high-volume service automation.

  • High-Automation Tier: Absorbs the 24% of your call volume with structured workflows (Follow-up, Payment, COIs, Policy Info Update).
  • Internal Knowledge Search: Instant retrieval from your 72+ organized policy and procedure files.
  • Investment: $5,000–$10,000/month (12-month commitment).

Implementation Timeline

Phase 1: Setup & Grounding (Days 1–7)

The first week ensures the Retention Associate operates from a "Single Source of Truth."

  • BT Core Integration: Connected App OAuth setup. Real-time access to policies, renewals, and claims.
  • Producer Walkthrough (30 min): Map the renewal-prep workflow per Producer.
  • Carrier-Exit Cohort: Main Street America and NGM Insurance prioritized as the first proof set.

Phase 2: First Cohort Live (Days 8–30)

Active deployment for the 30/60/90-day renewal cohorts.

  • Snapshot Generation: Per-account coverage, policies-by-carrier, claims activity, last-touch dates.
  • Pre-Review Email Drafts: Surfaced for Producer review, edit, and send.
  • ROI Review (Week 4): 15-min check-in with Brian and the AI Roundtable.

Phase 3: Expansion Decision (Days 31–60)

Once the Retention Associate has proven its ROI, we make the Phase 2 decision.

  • Carrier-Exit Outcomes: Reported across the prioritized cohort.
  • Producer Training: On edit/send patterns and persistence-cadence configuration.
  • Phase 2 Scope: Define full bilingual or English-first Lead Capture deployment.
